Our Mission

Our platform aggregates trending search data from 11 countries including the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, France, Norway, Sweden, Japan, South Korea, and Singapore - delivering multilingual explanations to make these trends accessible to everyone, regardless of language barriers.

How It Works

1. Data Collection: Our automated system monitors Google Trends across 11 countries every 3 hours, capturing the top 10 trending keywords from each region.

2. News Analysis: For each trending keyword, we gather related news articles from reputable sources to understand the context behind the trend.

3. AI Translation & Explanation: Using advanced AI language models, we generate concise, factual explanations in 7 different languages, focusing solely on verified information from news sources.

4. Real-Time Display: All data is instantly available on our platform, updated throughout the day to keep you informed of the latest global trends.
